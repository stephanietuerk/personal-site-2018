Pennsylvania Population Change

Understanding the Relationship Betweeen Trends in Population Change and the 2016 Election


Intro Visualization

For this data visualization project, we explore congressional district voting patterns. Most analysis break down election data by county. We took this county data, added census data, and attached voting patterns to census tract. From that, we rebuilt shapefiles into congressional districts in order to explore the changes at this level. This will allow us to explore how the districts are set up in order to determine if the congressmen accurately represents their district. This will be seen by looking at population changes over time.
